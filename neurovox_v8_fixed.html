Purpose of Data Collection

NeuroVox collects your voice recording and health questionnaire responses for the purpose of acoustic feature analysis and neurological disorder screening research. This is a prototype research tool developed at DPSRU and is not a certified medical device.

What We Collect

• Short voice recordings (up to 60 seconds)
• Acoustic feature data extracted from your voice
• Health history responses from the questionnaire
• Age group and language preference

Voice audio is processed on-device. Feature data (not raw audio) may be stored for research with your explicit consent below.

Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India), you have the right to: access your data, correct inaccuracies, withdraw consent at any time, and request erasure.

Legal Notice: This tool is intended solely for research and educational purposes. It does not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Results must not be used as a substitute for professional clinical evaluation. By proceeding, you acknowledge these limitations.
